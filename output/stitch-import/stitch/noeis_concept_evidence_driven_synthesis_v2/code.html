The Architecture of
Recursive Synthesis

Working Claim
"Intellectual labor is no longer a linear progression from source to conclusion, but a recursive loop where evidence actively refines the inquiry parameters in real-time."
Confidence: 0.89

Current paradigms of synthesis rely on the aggregation of existing knowledge. However, as the velocity of information increases, the 'static archive' becomes a liability. We must transition toward active-inference instruments that treat sources not as fixed data points, but as weights in a probabilistic model of truth.

How does the 'weight' of a source change when a counter-factual is introduced in a tangential field?

question_mark

The recursive nature of this process demands a new kind of interface—one that visualizes the contradiction density of a document. Rather than hiding friction, the interface should surface it as a primary metric for refinement. When two sources disagree, the gap between them is not an error, but a site of potential discovery.
