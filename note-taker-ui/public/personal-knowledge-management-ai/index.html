How to Use AI in Personal Knowledge Management

Personal knowledge management gets harder when capture is easy but synthesis is weak. AI is useful only when it improves retrieval, connection, and synthesis across your own material.

What AI should do in a PKM system

  • Help you retrieve the right context when you are writing or deciding.
  • Support connection across notes instead of creating more disconnected text.
  • Stay grounded in your material rather than generating detached output.
