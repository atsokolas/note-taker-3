# Traffic Ops Playbook (X + Reddit + Other Channels)

## Goal
Drive qualified users to Noeis daily via consistent posting + engagement.

## Daily structure
- **Morning brief**: plan the day (angles, channels, targets)
- **Session 1 (AM)**: 1 X post + X engagement + Reddit contribution
- **Session 2 (Midday)**: 1 X post + engagement + optional Reddit comment loop
- **Session 3 (PM)**: 1 X post + engagement + optional approved channel
- **Evening summary**: what was posted, what performed, next-day adjustments

## Channel strategy
### X
- Cadence: 3 posts/day max
- Mix:
  - 1 build-in-public update
  - 1 educational workflow post
  - 1 POV/contrarian insight
- Engagement target: 15–30 meaningful replies/day

### Reddit
- Focus on contribution-first in relevant communities (PKM, productivity, founders, AI tools)
- Prefer comments and context-rich posts over direct promotion
- Track subreddit-level response quality and remove low-signal attempts

### Other places (approved)
- Indie Hackers updates/comments
- Product Hunt discussions/comments
- Hacker News comments where highly relevant and non-promotional

## CTA hierarchy
