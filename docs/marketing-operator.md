## Canonical positioning
Noeis is a concept-centered thinking workspace for serious readers.

It helps people turn highlights, notes, and saved reading into maintained concepts, evidence-backed synthesis, and reusable insight.

## Category
Use language like:
- concept-centered thinking workspace
- personal research and synthesis workspace
- thinking system for serious readers

Avoid leading with:
- AI second brain
- generic note-taking app
- broad productivity tool

## Core audience
Primary:
- founders
- writers
- researchers
- analysts
- intellectually serious operators
- Readwise-heavy users and people with large reading archives

Secondary:
- grad students
- newsletter writers
- independent researchers
- skeptical AI users who still want human judgment preserved

## Core pains
- retrieval failure, not capture failure
- too much saved material, too little usable recall
- weak connection-making across prior reading
- generic AI writing tools that erase nuance and evidence

## Key messages
1. From fragments to concepts
2. Reliable recall, not just storage
3. Evidence stays attached
4. AI assists, you decide
5. Built for return

## Content pillars
